Eggy Rice

Description

Simple rice and egg comfort dish

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot white rice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon soy sauce, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon hot chili oil, plus more to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the egg sunny side up, until just thick enough to form solid structure
  2. Place rice in a bowl and fried egg on top. Add sesame oil, soy sauce and chili oil.
  3. Stir rice until egg is incorporated into rice
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper
